Fix 404 on published posts and content not loading in editor

Two fixes:

1. 404 on published posts: vercel.json rewrite /post/:slug didn't match
   URLs with trailing slash (/post/slug/). Added /post/:slug/ rewrite rule.
   Same fix for /go/:slug/.

2. Content not loading in editor: loadQuillContent was using
   clipboard.convert() which has a different API in Quill 2.0. Now uses
   dangerouslyPasteHTML() as primary method (most reliable in Quill 2),
   with convert() and innerHTML as fallbacks.
