Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Who we are

Eira ("we", "us") is a beauty, skincare, fashion and fitness affiliate blog. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use the site and how we handle it.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you subscribe to the newsletter. We use it solely to send the weekly email. We never sell or share it.
  • Click data — when you click an affiliate link (a /go/ link), we record the link, the referring page, and your browser's user-agent string for aggregate analytics. We do not store your IP address with clicks.

Cookies

The public site does not set tracking cookies. A session cookie is used only for the site administrator's login. Retailers you visit through our affiliate links (such as Amazon) set their own cookies to attribute the referral — see their privacy policies for details.

Affiliate links

As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. When you click an affiliate link and buy something, the retailer may pay us a commission at no extra cost to you. The retailer processes your purchase under its own privacy policy.

How long we keep data

Newsletter emails are kept until you unsubscribe. Aggregate click data is kept indefinitely for analytics.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete your email address or any data we hold about you at any time. Contact us via the contact page and we'll action it promptly. If you're in the UK/EU, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

Changes

We'll update this page if our data practices change, and update the date at the top.
